For Members

Creating an account

Use the Register link in the portal header. Depending on grid settings, your registration may require approval by a grid administrator before you can log in. You'll be notified once your account is active.

Logging in

Sign in with your in-world (OpenSimulator) account name and password using the Login link. The portal authenticates against the grid, so your portal login is the same identity you use in the viewer.

The first time grid policies are published or updated, you'll be asked to review and agree to them before the rest of the portal unlocks.

Your profile

The profile page (the person icon in the header) lets you review your account and change your password.

News, events, and who's online

  • Home / News — grid announcements and alerts.
  • Calendar — browse events by month, week, day, or schedule, follow events for reminders, and (where allowed) suggest community events.
  • Online — see who is currently in-world.
  • Documents & Policies — grid policies, guides, and resources.

Getting help

Open the Helpdesk (the ❓ icon) to file a support ticket. Guests can submit tickets too; a CAPTCHA protects the form from spam.

For Grid Admins

Admin tools appear in the Admin Tools dropdown when your account has the matching portal permissions.

Portal permissions are managed with a role/permission system separate from your in-world account level. The first login by a grid-owner account (OpenSimulator userLevel 250 or higher) is automatically granted Super Admin, which can then grant permissions to others under User Management.

Common admin areas

  • Registration Approvals — approve or decline pending sign-ups.
  • User Management — look up accounts, review notes, and manage bans.
  • Event Moderation — approve community-suggested events.
  • Region Configs — manage region settings and hosts.
  • Grid Policies — publish policies, guides, and resources.
  • System Settings — grid identity, theming, and backend options.
  • Bot Queue — inspect the in-world Grid Service Bot message queue.
  • Audit Log — review administrative actions.

Theming

Under System Settings you can point the portal at a custom stylesheet. The default stylesheet is /static/css/base.css.

Installation, deployment, and server operations are documented for administrators in the repository: see README.md, docs/DEPLOYMENT.md, and docs/OPERATIONS.md.

In-World & Support

Grid Service Bot

The portal can deliver messages and notices in-world through a Grid Service Bot driven by an LSL script. Event reminders and certain notifications are queued by the portal and delivered to avatars or groups in-world.

Calendar feeds

Events are also available as subscribable feeds:

  • /events/feed.ics — iCalendar feed for calendar apps.
  • /events/feed.rss — RSS feed for readers.

Reporting a problem

Use the Helpdesk to open a ticket. Include what you were doing, what you expected, and what happened. Admins can reply and track the ticket to resolution.
